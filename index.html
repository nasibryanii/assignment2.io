What about us?

Anytime fitness is a franchise of 24-hour health and fitness clubs that is headquartered in Woodbury, Minnesota, United States. The company operates over 4,000 franchised locations in 50 countries. The gym facilities are open 24 hours a day, 365 days of the year.

Anytime Fitness gym has 76 branches in Singapore to choose from, spread evenly across!

With a membership, open to both men and women, you can visit any of the chain’s locations without any extra costs. That means you can probably (given the thousands (5,000+) of international locations) get your work out at Anytime Fitness even on your holidays and work trips abroad.
